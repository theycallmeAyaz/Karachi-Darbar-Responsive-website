Our Cuisine

Embark on a Culinary Journey with us and explore your tastebuds.

  • Chicken Biryani

    A fragrant and flavorful rice dish cooked with chicken and aromatic spices.Each grain of rice is infused with the essence of the spices, creating a dish that's rich in taste and texture.

  • Tandoori Chicken

    A traditiona dish marinated in a blend of yogurt and spices, then roasted in a tandoor until tender and succulent. The high heat of the tandoor imparts a smoky flavor and a beautifully charred exterior to the chicken.

  • Nihari

    A slow-cooked stew made with tender cuts of meat (often beef or lamb), simmered with spices until it reaches a melt-in-your-mouth consistency. Nihari is typically enjoyed with naan or rice and is known for its rich and hearty flavor.

  • Chicken Tikka Masala

    A popular dish of roasted chunks of chicken (tikka) cooked in a creamy tomato-based sauce, flavored with a blend of Indian spices. It's a comforting and indulgent dish with a perfect balance of tangy and spicy flavors.

  • Palak Paneer

    A vegetarian dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a creamy spinach sauce, seasoned with garlic, ginger, and aromatic spices. It's a nutritious and satisfying dish that's packed with flavor and goodness.

  • Seekh Kebabs

    These are minced meat kebabs seasoned with spices, herbs, and aromatics, then shaped onto skewers and grilled to perfection. Seekh kebabs are known for their smoky flavor and juicy texture, making them a favorite appetizer or snack.
